# Contact

DisplayHive is under active development — if something's broken, confusing,
or missing, here's where to reach out.

- **Bug reports & feature requests** — open an issue on
  [GitHub](https://github.com/DisplayHive/DisplayHive/issues).
- **Questions, feedback, or just saying hi** — reach us on Mastodon at
  `displayhive@chaos.social`.
- **Contributing a change** — see the [Contributing](developer/contributing.md)
  guide for how PRs are reviewed and what's expected of them.

!!! tip "Not sure where something belongs?"
    When in doubt, open a GitHub issue — it's easier for us to track and
    follow up on than a social media message.
